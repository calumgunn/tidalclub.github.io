Tidal Club

Free meetups for people interested in TidalCycles, the live coding language for exploring musical pattern. We do informal demos, hands-on experiments and peer-learning accessible to total beginners as well as the more experienced. People new to code and/or music are welcome.

Presented by Access Space Labs + SONA + FoAM
